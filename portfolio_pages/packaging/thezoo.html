 | PACKAGING | THE ZOO WINERY

Unbox your Party Animal! The Zoo Winery is a fictitious brand that believes humans are not so different than animals when they let loose a little. The type of wine is paired with a specific animal so when the consumer finds their favorite wine they also get to meet their Party Animal excited for a fun night with others. The design inspiration for Party Animal wine came from zoo crates which carry animals to and from the parks. The wine is placed in a wooden box with the labels representing the shipping stamps. With The Zoo Winery, the consumer is asked to let loose and unbox their Party Animal to have a night of fun!
